JUNO / KOTO Joint Workshop @ NKNU

This workshop brings together researchers and students from institutions across Taiwan working on the JUNO and KOTO experiments. The goal is to exchange recent progress, review current challenges, and explore opportunities for long-term collaboration. Topics include detector development, data analysis, and instrumentation, providing a platform to strengthen connections among domestic research teams and foster future joint research efforts.
